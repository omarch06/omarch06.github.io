My goal

I started off with wanting to be a PE teacher in high school, but messed up since entering college. I was supposed to do Kinesiology major instead of liberal studies, because it would be more tailored towards my end goal.

So when I went to meet with my counselor, that's when I started taking classes that weren't related to my field. When I transferred to Fresno State, I realized that I couldn't go on throught the kinesiology major and had to stick with liberal studies. I was not thrilled.

I chose to pursue a new career path instead, and that is where I currently am. I would still like to become a HS PE teacher someday, but I realized that I also want to be a web developer. I hope to get my degree through Fresno City and then pursue a higher education or take courses online (that are respected). I hope to build a portfolio that catches the attention of a potential employer.

One other goal I have is to move somewhere else, whether that be somewhere else in town or to a completely different state. Or country (Nah, I'm good here). But my goal is to get a more than respectable job that allows me to get an awesome house. But I also have told myself repeatedly that once I get a respectable job, that I will take great care of my parents first. I have to repay them in the highest possible way for putting up with my crap for so many years.

My final goal would be to own my own business. I have been thinking about either having my own gym or having a company that hires Jr. developers for their first job and also trains/challenges them to move on to that new and higher paying job that they may or may not be seeking. I would obviously keep it local, but if an opportunity arises where I can expand my business, the I'm all in.

I cannot predict my future. I have so many aspirations, and am trying to hone in on some to make my dreams a reality. All I know is that God is always in control and that the future he has for me is the future that I will have to learn to love. #Godsplan
